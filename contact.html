â“ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I collect my order?
After submitting your order, you'll receive a receipt with an order number. Bring this receipt to our office, make payment, and collect your items.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash, M-Pesa, and Bank Transfer at our office. Payment is made when you collect your order.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
Yes! Contact us immediately if you need to cancel or modify your order before it's verified. Call our support line or visit our office.
How long does order verification take?
Most orders are verified within 24 hours during working days. You can check your order status in your profile page.
Do you offer delivery services?
Currently, all orders must be collected from our office. We're working on delivery services for the future!
